Chai

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 3 adults
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 cup
Water 2 cups
Tea Leaves 3 tbsp
Sugar/Agave 3 tbsp
Ginger 1/2 tsp

This warm chai will give your friends and family a warm feeling in their heart and head, as this chai is a headache-soothing and heartwarming drink to make for your loved ones. With only ten minutes of prep, your guests will surely love this drink!

Steps:

  1. Gather a pot and 3 cups for chai. You will also need a drainer, along with the listed ingredients above.
  2. Place the pot on the stove and set to medium-high. Pour in the water and the milk.
  3. Put in the tea leaves and chop up some ginger and place that in as well.
  4. Let everything boil for about 3 minutes. Make sure you don't let it sit for too long, otherwise the chai will spill out of the pot! To prevent this, put the stove on low for 2 minutes and let it simmer.
  5. After 2 minutes, grab your pot and set the drainer on top of the cup and pour chai into the cup. Repeat this 3 times for all cups.
